About

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a disorder involving infrequent, irregular or prolonged menstrual periods, and often excess male hormone (androgen) levels. The ovaries develop numerous small collections of fluid called follicles and may fail to regularly release eggs. Ever thought if you were a woman having PCOS! Let's have the perspective & thoughts of that female, feel the pain and shyness that girl is going through!

Provides a portal for community bonding where other PCOS Patients can interact and help each other with the best yoga and food practices. They can also write the blogs in the community section.


Steps for analysing PCOS

Step 1

You might have Irregular, heavy, or missed periods due to missed ovulation—the release of an egg from your ovaries.

Step 2

You might have unwanted hair growth on your face—especially the upper lip and on the chin, back, chest, and in other places where men typically grow hair. You might lose hair or see it thinning.

Step 3

You might develop acne on your face, chest, and upper back, and put on extra weight, particularly around your middle.

Step 4

You could notice dark skin in the creases of your armpits or on your neck. Sometimes you might have mood swings.
